XG

Debut: 2022

  • XG is a Japanese girl group based in South Korea. The group was formed by Xgalx, a label of Avex, and is composed of seven members: Jurin, Chisa, Hinata, Juria, Cocona, Maya and Harvey. They made their debut on March 18, 2022, with the release of the digital single "Tippy Toes"
